Give Every Meal a Second Chance.

Reduce Food Waste. Feed Communities.

ReBite connects restaurants, volunteers, and NGOs to rescue surplus food and deliver it where it is needed.

How ReBite Works

1. Restaurants Post Food

Restaurants add surplus food with quantity and pickup location.

2. Volunteers Claim Delivery

Volunteers accept available donations and deliver them safely.

3. NGOs Receive Meals

NGOs get confirmed deliveries and distribute meals to people in need.
